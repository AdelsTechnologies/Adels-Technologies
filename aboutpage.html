About Me

Adel's Technologies was founded in the year 4th September, 2022, by Olanrewaju Samuel Oluwaseun, porpularly known as Adels. He is a young and vibrant man with great love for tech, his coding carrer started after a unwanted incident that happened to him in the city of Abuja which forced him to move to Lagos. There he discovered his passion for tech and decided to venture fully into it.

After few months in the city of Lagos and he moved back to Abuja with the aim of developing his tech skill with the goal of becoming a Frontend Web Developer someday. He is a graduate of Public Administration from The Federal Polytechnic Bida, Niger State, Nigeria, and also a graduate of Business Administration from The National Open University of Nigeria, a Diploma Holder in Computer, a Professional Video Editor, Animator, Graphics Designer and many more tech related skills.
